I’m Professor of Cardiovascular Physiology and Pharmacology at UCL and Head of the Research Department of Population Science & Experimental Medicine in the UCL Institute of Cardiovascular Science. I also lead the Heart-Brain Theme of the BHF Centre of Research Excellence at UCL.

My research interests include haemodynamics, wave intensity analysis, blood pressure and cardiometabolic health.
