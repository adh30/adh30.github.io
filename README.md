I'm Professor of Cardiovascular Physiology and Pharmacology at UCL. I am also Head of the Department of [Population Science & Experimental Medicine](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/cardiovascular/research/research-department-population-science-and-experimental-medicine) in [UCL Institute of Cardiovascular Science](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/cardiovascular/ucl-institute-cardiovascular-science), Director of the British Heart Foundation 4-year PhD programme at UCL, and Associate Director of the [MRC Unit for Lifelong Health and Ageing at UCL](https://www.ucl.ac.uk/cardiovascular/research/population-science-and-experimental-medicine/mrc-unit-lifelong-health-and-ageing-ucl).

My research interests include haemodynamics, wave intensity analysis,  blood pressure and cardiovascular health.
